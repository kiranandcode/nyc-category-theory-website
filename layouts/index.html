About the Reading Club

Are you interested in diving into the world of Category Theory? Join our NYC Beginners-friendly Reading Club!

Goal: Learn category theory!
Who: Anyone interested in learning category theory, beginner friendly!
What: Discussion of sections of the book, exercises and insights. Progress is non-linear so feel free to drop in even if you missed previous meetings.

We are hoping to keep the mathematical requirements low, though you might want to grab a copy of the book in advance and check it fits your taste.
